Cripsy Golden Chicken Wings

Description

A very simple recipe for cooking the perfect chicken wings.

ingredients

  • As many chicken wings as you want
  • Salt (optional)

For Roasting

  1. Pre-heat an oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Take chicken wings out of packaging.
  3. Place in an oven proof stainless steal dish.
  4. Place wings in the oven and cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Once almost golden flip them and cook for another 15 to 20 minutes depending on how golden and cripsy you want them.
  6. When they reach your desired finish, remove from oven, give them a light salting if you want salt and allow them to rest for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve and enjoy, if you eat dairy serve with sour cream.

For Grilling on a Fire

  1. Once your coals or gas flames are at your desired heat, remove chicken wings from their packaging.
  2. Place wings evenly on the grill and let them grill for 10 to 15 minutes depending on how hot your heat source is.
  3. Once they've turned golden, flip them over and grill for another 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. When they reach your preferred golden crisp, remove from grill, give them a salting if you want salt and let them rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Server and enjoy, again if you eat dairy serve with sour cream.

Additional Notes on Cooking Times

The only thing that will vary cooking times are your preferences and the heat at which you cook them.

Some days I like my chicken wings skin still soft and so I roast/grill them for less on each side.

On the days I feel like my chicken wings skin a little more crispy I simply cook them a little longer, or at at higher temperature.

This isn't rocket science, it's chicken wings you can throw them on a skewer and suspend them over an open flame for long enough and have a lovely done wing, experiment and have fun.
